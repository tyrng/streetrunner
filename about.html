About Street Runner Express

Street Runner Express Services Sdn. Bhd. is a courier service company headquartered in Setapak, Kuala Lumpur which does courier service throughout West Malaysia. The company currently has 12 branches to serve each state in West Malaysia.

Unlike traditional courier services, the company is the first in Malaysia that can pickup customers' items from their doorsteps and deliver them.

The idea was inspired by foreign courier companies like FedEx and Amazon.
